Why Choose Us

We Are Professional Logistics & cargo Agency

At our logistics company, we excel in providing specialized transportation services using Box trucks, Flatbeds and Dry Vans. Our reliable and efficient solutions cater to businesses of all sizes, accommodating the transportation of both small packages and oversized cargo. The comprehensive range of services we offer includes:

  • Box truck transportation: Our box trucks are perfectly suited for the transportation of small to medium-sized cargo, encompassing items such as packages, furniture, and equipment.

  • Flatbed transportation: flatbed trucks are the ideal choice when it comes to transporting bulky, heavy, or oversized cargo that cannot be accommodated by a standard box truck.

  • Dry van transportation: Designed specifically to safeguard goods from external elements, our dry van trucks are the optimal solution for transporting items like electronics, clothing, and paper products.

Requirements

To deliver service of exceptional quality

Our transportation of goods entails meeting specific requirements, which encompass regulatory compliance, precise documentation, and adherence to our stringent safety protocols. We offer tailor-made solutions and maintain close collaboration with you to ensure that all your requirements are met effectively.

Read More

  • Copy of CDL's

    Before we can transport your goods, we require a copy of the commercial driver's license (CDL) of the driver who will be operating the vehicle.

  • Certified state

    We require that all vehicles used to transport goods are certified by the state where they are registered.

  • Minimum Insurance

    To protect your goods and ensure that you are covered in case of any accidents or incidents, we require a minimum level of insurance coverage for all shipments.

  • Sign 9-W

    The 9-W form is a document that verifies that the goods being transported are not subject to hazardous materials regulations.

  • Signed Carrier Agreement

    We require a signed carrier agreement between our logistics company and the client before we can transport any goods.
